About us

BeautyShop is India’s fastest-growing beauty and cosmetics brand that aims to be every makeup lover’s BFF. With an in-depth understanding of Indian Beauty needs, BeautyShop integrates global cosmetic standards to create products that are highly innovative, long wear, and comfortable. While elevating beauty to Swiss standards, we offer an unparalleled experience through high- functioning game changing products. As a brand, BeautyShop believes makeup is for everyone and we celebrate the individuality of every Indian - unstoppable, unique and fiercely expressive.

Incepted in 2013, BeautyShop is the brainchild of Mr. Amit & Mohit Goyal which soon became India’s favourite beauty and cosmetics brand which believes in elevating the Indian beauty to Swiss standards. BeautyShop, is inspired out of the sheer innovation and standards of perfection from Switzerland, combine this to international cosmetic standards with an in-depth understanding of the Indian beauty needs, BeautyShop offers an unparalleled experience through products that are ideal for a variety of Indian skin tones. BeautyShop believes every Indian girl is unique
and can don her individuality with all the sass and confidence she wants.

In the coming years, BeautyShop plans to consolidate its leadership position in color cosmetics further and cater to sub-categories of premium and professional requirements. It will include introducing new categories like body care and grooming tools.
